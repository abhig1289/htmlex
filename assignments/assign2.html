Rohit sharma


rohit sharma is a right hand opening batsmen for indian cricket team in all three formats.He is considered to be one of the legends of white ball cricket of all time for his assured timing and class that his bat display at the highest level. He represents mumbai indians as a captain in ipl and he was most successful captain in ipl history with 5 titles under his captaicy.

Sharma holds the world record for the highest individual score by a batsman playing in a one-day international match, having scored 264 against Sri Lanka at Eden Gardens, Kolkata, on 13 November 2014.[4] He is the only player to have scored three double-centuries in this form of international cricket.[86] In January 2020, Sharma was named as the ODI Player of the Year by the International Cricket Council (ICC).[87] During the 2019 World Cup, Sharma became the only batsman to scored five centuries in a single edition of the Cricket World Cup.[88] On 5 October 2019, during a Test match against South Africa, Sharma became the first batsman to score two centuries in a match on his first appearance as an opening batsman.[89] In the same series, he broke Shimron Hetmyer's record for the highest number of sixes in a Test series
